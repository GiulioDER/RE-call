# Hypotheses

## H-014 — Prompt-injected context improves answers (CLOSED, FALSIFIED)
We tested injecting retrieved context directly into the model prompt. Across
nine paradigms it reduced answer quality versus retrieval-as-tool. Conclusion:
do not inject retrieved context into the prompt. Status: closed.

## H-021 — Reranking helps on short queries (OPEN)
Early signal suggests cross-encoder reranking lifts precision on 1-3 word
queries. Not yet confirmed. Status: open, collecting data.
